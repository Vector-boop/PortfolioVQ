About Me

Me

My name is Victor Quaedvlieg, and I'm a 22-year-old student at Avans University of Applied Sciences, currently in my third year of the Communication and Multimedia Design program. I'm passionate about the intersection of storytelling and technology, where creativity meets innovation.

Strengths

My strengths lie in 3D design, photography, and videography, through which I explore new ways to craft engaging, immersive experiences. I enjoy translating ideas into visually compelling narratives that connect with audiences on both emotional and conceptual levels.

Goals

Looking ahead, my goal is to design interactive products and create immersive visual experiences at a professional level. I aim to continue developing my technical expertise while refining my ability to communicate stories that leave a lasting impact through design, motion, and interactivity.
