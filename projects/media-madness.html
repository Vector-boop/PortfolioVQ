MEDIA MADNESS

A short movie both criticizing modern work ethic, attention spans and unethical media practices. MEDIA MADNESS was a 1 minute satire short-story I made to critique the need for redundant discussion and stereotypes current prominent Dutch media figures. We had a lot of fun building up a studio environment and writing a relevant and engaging storyline. This was perhaps my favourite project because it most reflected the creative direction I want to go, and taught me a lot. I was primarily responsible for planning, editing and camerawork.
