AR You Ready

Game Design Immersive Design Speculative Design Storytelling

Concept

"AR You Ready" was a project commissioned by the PBL Netherlands Environmental Assessment Agency, which sought to emulate one of the four scenarios outlined in the report Vier scenario's voor de inrichting van Nederland in 2050. The game offers an immersive experience that allows players to explore the Dutch living environment in 2050 through the eyes of a journalist. By stepping into a potential future scenario, it provides a realistic glimpse into the everyday life of the average Dutch citizen.

Responsibilities

For AR You Ready, I was primarily responsible for designing and creating a diverse yet modular building environment inspired by the architectural character of Utrecht. My research focused on Dutch Neo-Renaissance architecture, which I integrated with elements of futuristic urban planning to craft an aesthetic that both reflected the setting and reinforced the project's narrative.
